# Origin

This repo exists to show that Kinetic Gain's control-plane pattern extends cleanly into biotech and diagnostics safety operations.

The primitive is not generic "drug safety monitoring." The real workflow pressure lives in:

- incomplete case intake packets that still need seriousness rationale
- medical review and MedDRA coding drift that can distort signal quality
- overdue follow-up and literature evidence that weakens escalation
- aggregate reporting and named committee actions that determine whether the board can trust the packet

`pharmacovigilance-signal-router` packages that pressure into a buyer-readable and operator-readable public surface.
